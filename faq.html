2. What's with the Quality?
It's important to understand that "Quality" thing you see on the info page(also known as "prints").

1. HDCAM - Low quality - It's usually available right from the day of release. HDCAM is for the people who just can't wait.

2. HDTC - Low/Medium quality - It's a bit better than HDCAM and "watchable" as we like to call it.

3. HDRIP - High quality - This is where the game starts. Most people wait for this print because it has high quality audio and video.

4. WEB-DL - Excellent quality - This is available when the movie/series is available on streaming platforms such PrimeVideo, Netflix, etc. 

5. BluRay - Outstanding quality - This is best you can get.

All in all,
HDCAM/HDTC - For people who can't wait.
HDRIP/WEB-DL/BluRay - For quality lovers.

 

3. Link is always opening in Drive app?
We don't recommend the use of Drive app because it comes with its own flaws. Now, there are 2 ways to stop Drive app from snatching the link:

1. Fixing the Default behaviour: (Recommended)
Watch the video above or follow these steps -
i. Open Settings > Apps.
ii. Search for "Drive" app.
iii. Click on "Open by default" (or similar).
iv. Click on "Open supported links in the app".
v. Choose "Ask every time". That's it!

2. Disabling/Uninstalling the Drive app:
In case if the above method doesn't work, you can disable the "Drive" app entirely -
i. Open Settings > Apps.
ii. Search for "Drive" app.
iii. Click on "Disable" or "Uninstall".

NOTE: These steps might not be exactly the same depending on the Android version and UI!

 

4. How to Download movies?
--Night Owl now supports 1-Click download--

*Following instructions are for the movies added before v8*
1. Click on the "Download" button.
2. Choose a browser.
3. Click on "Login to Download".
4. Allow Drivebox to link with your Google account.
5. Click on "Download" on the next page.

NOTES:
1. Drivebox comes with its own ads. It's recommended to use a browser with an ad-blocker.
2. Drivebox copies the file to your Drive account so you should always delete those movies (incl. from trash) and make sure you have enough space in your Google Drive.

5. How to Watch Online/Stream?
--Night Owl now supports 1-click Streaming for movies--

Night Owl uses an external video player to stream and for that a supported video player is required (works best with VLC Media Player).

*Following instructions are for the movies added before v8*
For Movies:
1. Follow the Download process.
2. Stop at the final page and just copy the link.
2. Click on "Watch Now" and paste the link.
4. Again click on "Watch Now" and Enjoy!

For Series:
1. Click on the "Download" button.
2. Select season.
3. Choose your desired episode.
4. Click on the Download button.
5. Stop at the final page and just copy the link.
6. Click on "Watch Now" and paste the link.
7. Again click on "Watch Now" and Enjoy!

10. What about Subtitles?
Most of the Movies/Series on Night Owl already come with Subtitles but you can always add subtitles manually using the following methods:

1. MX Player-
i. Play the video.
ii. Click on the top-right 3 dot menu button.
iii. Click on "Subtitle".
iv. Click on "Online Subtitles".
v. Click on "Search".
vi. Tick the "Enter your search text" checkbox.
vii. Just enter the movie name.
viii. Choose a subtitle and click on "Download".

2. VLC Player-
i. Play the video.
ii. Click on the subtitles button.
iii. Click on "Download subtitles".
iv. Tick the "Manual search" check box.
v. Enter the details.
vi. Select a suitable subtitle.

3. You can also use any 3rd party service such as "Get Subtitles" app.
