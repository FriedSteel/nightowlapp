Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What's with the Quality?
It's important to understand that "Quality" thing you see on the info page. They're most commonly known as "prints".

1. HDCAM - Low quality - It's available right from the day of release. HDCAM is for the people who just can't wait.

2. HDTC - Low/Medium quality - It's a bit better than HDCAM and "watchable" as we like to call it.

3. HDRIP - High quality - This is where the game starts. Most people wait for this print because it has high quality audio and video.

4. HDTV - High/Great quality - HDTV is rare and quite similar to HDRIP.

5. WEB-HD/WEB-DL - Excellent quality - This is available when the movie/series is available on streaming platforms such Prime, Netflix, etc. 

6. BluRay - Outstanding quality - This may take anywhere between 6 to 12 months and this is best you can get.

All in all, HDCAM/HDTC - For people who can't wait.
            HDTV/HDRIP/WEB-DL/BluRay - For quality lovers.

2. Why only 720p?

whereas 1080p movies easily go above 2GB. The difference between 720p and 1080p is not significant especially in phones.
