  2. If your account gets cancelled within 5 days then you're eligible for free replacement.
  3. If the account gets cancelled after 5 days then you can either choose to get a Refund for the remaining days OR buy a new account with discounted price (for the remaining days).
  4. You are allowed to change the password.
  5. You are allowed to enable profile lock.
  6. The warranty will be void if:
    i. You change the Email id.
    ii. Logged in to more than 4 screens.
    iii. You change any account setting (other than password).
    iv. You forget your password or any similar case.
    v. Create more than 4 profiles.

Mandatory Precautions:
Strictly follow these instructions before logging in:

For Android

For iOS

PC
